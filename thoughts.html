About Our Project

The internet is a place to share and exchange information. However your data is important and you need to know everything about how to protect it.

Why Should You Care About Your Privacy

Your personal information is just that, personal. It is important to know how your data is being used and what you can do to limit companies’ access to your private information. Your data can be sold, it can be used to manipulate you, it can be used to predict your next purchases. As a teenager, you are just beginning to transition into adulthood, and with that comes a responsibility to secure your online presence.

We are teenagers too. We created this website to inform our peers about how valuable data really is. Learn more about us below!
