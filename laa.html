Look Alike Analysis

What is Look Alike Analysis?

Companies use Look Alike Analysis to expand their audiences and identify their best users so that they can target them. Companies typically deem the users who engage most often with their website and content, or at least those who are most likely to do so, as their “best customers.”

Why is Look Alike Analysis Beneficial?

This analysis can be used by companies to optimize their budget, potentially even saving money. It can also help them allocate their resources better by dedicating them to users who they are more likely to benefit from. Most importantly, companies benefit from look alike analysis by providing them with a better understanding of their users, which enables them to base their company on the user’s interests and convenience.

How Does It Work?

To identify the best users, the company first sends out a list of device identifiers. Next, the company will analyze the traits of the best users. They do this by building up feature vectors. These feature vectors tell the company what device the users are on, what ads the user gets, what time of day the user is typically active, the user’s location, etc. After determining the traits of the best users, the company goes through their user database to identify all the users who share the same traits as the best users. The company then targets these users for promotion.
