Legal Aspects of Online Privacy

The Law Protects You

There are multiple privacy laws in place to protect users like you. Online privacy is protected by laws that date back to the seventies. There are a multitude of regulations that keep users and their information safe..

The US Privacy Act of 1974:

United States citizens have the right to access any data held by government agencies and a right to copy that data. They have the right to correct any information errors. Access to information is on a need to know basis. Sharing information between other federal (and non federal) agencies is restricted.
